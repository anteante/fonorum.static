Tveit, Anders

1977 Oslo, Norway

Improviser and Electro/Acoustic Composer.

Been working mainly in the Electro/Acoustic field of music, with a focus on free improvisation and experimental music since 2002.

Works with different concepts and approaches to electric bassplaying and Laptop software. Where the use of customized software and both conventional/unconventional playing techniques plays a large part of the personal musical expression.

Composed Electroacoustic music and sound design for multimedia, theater and for dance act.

Performances at festivals such as:

Høvikodden Kunstsenter 2005, Die Klangfest 2005 & 2007 in Liechtenstein, Elektronische Nacht at die Hochschüle fur musik und Darstellende Kunst Stuttgart,Germany 2006. Neue Musik,Feldkirch, Austria
Düsseldorf Jazz Rally 2007. Ultimafestival 2008, Oslo and more..
