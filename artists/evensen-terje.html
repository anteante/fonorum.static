Evensen, Terje

1978-2000

Born one winter’s morning, went to school, played soccer, took some drum lessons.
Tutored by Audun Kleive, Espen Ruud, Bjørn Kjellemyr, Paolo Vinaccia, and Audun Erlien

Composed music, gigged with own trio: Evensen Trio.

2001

Moved to London, gigging around town
Tutored by Martin France and John Marshal

2002

Living in London, gigging around town in various venues incl. Ronnie Scott’s
Gigging in Germany, Norway, and Austria.

2003

Released Andrés Prado Trio for Andrés Prado Trio.
Toured Peru incl. Lima International Jazz Festival.
Gigging in Norway, Germany, England with Julian Argüelles Quartett et.al.
Mixed Andrés Prado Trio together with Erik Skarsbø.
Moved back to Norway, joined STEREO Studio with Geir Bremer Øvrebø
Awarded the “Up and Coming” prize by the city of Skien, Norway.
