Good bye CD

With the last statement on fonorum.com we left our music label status for good. Taking a step further as a music platform, we not only heralded CDs as obsolete, but also developed the concept of the fonorum netalbum as a direct consequence. In June 2010, we celebrated the realisation of the netalbum with the release of Terje Evensens’ solo album Still You. You Still here.

fonorum • Ingvo Clauder & Lucas Dietrich • Berlin – June 14, 2010
