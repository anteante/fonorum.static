fonorum relaunch

more content less packaging

the label as we know it has become obsolete in the last years.
the discussion in autumn 2006 in stuttgart between ingvo and lucas is now materializing in form of a complete redesign of the fonorum online venue and as a result fonorum is a music-platform rather than a label & edition.
